Why we are starting
SmartDine

Introducing Smart Dine, the AI-powered tool transforming restaurant management which is designed to tackle challenges like inventory, staffing, and customer demand.

"Smart Dine" is designed to help restaurant managers tackle challenges related to inventory, staffing, and customer demand. By leveraging AI technology, the tool provides accurate customer demand predictions, allowing restaurants to optimize food preparation and staffing schedules.

The primary goal of "Smart Dine" is to reduce food waste, which not only saves money but also benefits the environment. By preventing overproduction and spoilage, the restaurant industry can lower its greenhouse gas emissions and contribute to a more sustainable future.

Beyond inventory management, "Smart Dine" helps optimize staff scheduling to avoid overstaffing during slow periods and understaffing during peak times. This leads to improved efficiency, better service, and greater employee satisfaction, reinforcing the mission of making restaurants more eco-friendly, profitable, and efficient.
